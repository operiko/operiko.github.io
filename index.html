The problem

Orchestration shouldn't be this hard.

Every team eventually hits the same wall. The tools that got you started can't scale with you. The tools built for enterprise are overkill for everyone else. You end up stitching things together with duct tape and hope.

Operiko is being designed to close that gap — one platform that makes sense at every stage, without forcing you to migrate, re-architect, or rewire your entire stack when you grow.
